# v0.2 Distillation Plan

v0.2 should promote only a small number of candidate rules into official cards.

## Promotion Limit

- v0.2 may add at most 7 official cards.
- Do not add theory terms just to sound advanced.
- Every promoted card must improve route selection, Agent instruction quality, or review quality.

## Promotion Requirements

Each promoted card must include:

- Trigger Condition
- Judgment Question
- Decision Rule
- Output Impact
- Misuse Risk

## Promotion Criteria

A candidate card may enter official `cards/` only if:

- It clearly improves output in at least 2 test cases.
- It affects RoutePlan, TaskSpec, or ReviewReport.
- It has a clear trigger condition.
- It does not duplicate an existing official card.
- It is not only a theory concept.

## Candidate Selection Process

1. Test each candidate against the 5 examples.
2. Keep candidates that change the recommended route or first action.
3. Reject candidates that only rename existing rules.
4. Prefer rules that reduce risk, reduce ambiguity, or reduce rework.
5. Promote no more than 7 cards.

## Likely v0.2 Candidates

- 目标函数先定义
- 硬约束不可违反
- 事实和假设分离
- 概念不清先定义
- 推荐必须有理由链
- 防止工具光环
- 高风险先列未知项

## Not Allowed

- No copied book passages
- No long theory essays
- No formulas without project-route impact
- No cards that require external data
- No cards that encourage automatic execution
